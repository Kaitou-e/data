Overall information

This is an endgame level 100 account that has the full Black Fang, Mira/Dark Tower, Duelist, and Triceratops sets and has 2/3 pieces of the Draconyx set. It has 6 level 100 pets. Includes the Epic Diveodile, which is now unobtainable. Furniture has a total worth of 1,911 titan shards, including 311 leftover titan shards. Buddies include Diveodile, Baby Triceratops, and Robo-Pup.

Diveodile Epic, buddy, and pet

The Diveodile Epic was a limited purchasable toy, which also included the digital in-game epic. The toy line was discontinued in late 2019, but the digital products purchased before that date still remains.
The Diveodile Epic's original value was $60. But, now that it is now unobtainable, the value has risen.

Mira/Dark Tower set

The Mira/Dark Tower set is a legendary set of equippment only obtainable by completing the Dark Tower. The Dark Tower is a 100 floored dungeon limited to Prodigy players with a membership only. There are checkpoints every 5 floors and the player has to progress through every level with only one team HP bar. Only a small precentage of players have completed the Dark Tower.

Baby Triceratops buddy

The Baby Triceratops is a limied buddy only obtainable by the top 1000 players in the Arena. It was the grand prize for the Triceratops season in 2018.
